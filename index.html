What are Dynamic Pools?

Dynamic pools are a type of liquidity pool that uses automated market makers (AMMs) to facilitate trading and lending on decentralized exchanges (DEXs). Unlike traditional pools, dynamic pools dynamically allocate assets to external lending protocols to generate yield and rewards, reducing reliance on liquidity mining and creating a more sustainable liquidity provision for the ecosystem.

What is a DLMM?

A DLMM is an advanced liquidity management system that enhances capital efficiency and reduces slippage. Unlike traditional AMMs, DLMMs allow LPs to concentrate liquidity in specific price bins, ensuring zero slippage and maximizing earnings.

Key Features of Meteora’s DLMM

  • Zero Slippage: Trades within active bins have no price impact.
  • Dynamic Fees: Fees rise with market volatility, increasing LP earnings.
  • Concentrated Liquidity: LPs can allocate liquidity precisely to maximize fee capture.
  • Advanced Strategies: Utilize spot, curve, and bid-ask strategies to optimize earnings.
